Our Story

consulting.sa was founded with a simple yet ambitious vision: to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and practical business applications. We recognized that while AI technology was advancing rapidly, many businesses struggled to understand how to leverage these innovations effectively.

Starting as a small consulting practice in Saudi Arabia, we've grown into a trusted partner for businesses across the region and beyond. Our success is built on a foundation of deep technical expertise, practical business acumen, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success.

Today, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible with AI, helping our clients not just adopt new technologies, but transform their entire approach to business in the digital age.
